SoulGarden · PR Strategy

Why PR Newswire? The Logo Trust Strategy.

Distribution across premium outlets gives us something powerful: the credibility of their brand equity.

◆

Instant Trust Transfer — Users see Forbes, Bloomberg, TechCrunch and their trust threshold drops immediately.

◆

Accelerates Conversion — "As seen in" logos reduce sign-up friction and boost landing page CVR.

◆

Partnership Currency — Media logos open doors with investors, partners, and enterprise clients.

◆

Evergreen Asset — One press run generates logos we display across all marketing, forever.

This is the playbook. SoulGarden will have its own logo wall.
